Smart Medication Reminder Box

A calm, caring way to never miss a dose.

Problem

Elderly patients often forget to take medicines on time. Phone alarms can be missed and there is no confirmation whether the medicine was taken.

Solution

A smart pillbox that reminds using light and sound, connects to a mobile app, and tracks medication intake.
